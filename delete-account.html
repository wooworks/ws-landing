Account Deletion Guide

You can delete your Whatsub account and all associated data by following the steps below:

  • 1. Open the Whatsub app and navigate to Settings > Delete Account.
  • 2. Once the deletion request is confirmed, all personal data stored in Supabase will be permanently deleted and cannot be recovered.
  • 3. If you experience any issues or have questions, please contact us at team@whatsub.xyz.

What will be deleted:

  • - Your email address and unique authentication ID (auth_id)
  • - All registered subscription data
  • - Any referral codes linked to your account

Data Retention Policy:

All user data is deleted immediately and permanently upon request. No backups are stored or retained once deletion is confirmed.
